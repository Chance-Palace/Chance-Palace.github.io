About Hotel Chance Palace

Nestled in the beautiful town of Veyangoda, Hotel Chance Palace has established itself as the region's most trusted venue for weddings and special events. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create unforgettable experiences that celebrate life's most precious moments.

Located at Mallehewa Road, Kottala, Veyangoda 11100, our venue combines natural beauty with modern sophistication. We take pride in our attention to detail, exceptional service, and commitment to making every event truly special.

Our Mission

We strive to transform your vision into reality, providing a seamless blend of elegance, functionality, and personalized service. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we ensure every moment is crafted to perfection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?

Simply fill out our contact form or call us at 077 159 1342. Our event coordinators will discuss your requirements and schedule a venue visit.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 60+ days before receive a full refund. 30-60 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: deposits are non-refundable.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings, especially during peak season (November-April).

Do you provide catering?

Yes! We have in-house catering with customizable menus including Sri Lankan, Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines.

Can we bring our own decorators?

Yes, you're welcome to work with your preferred decorators. We also have partnerships with excellent companies.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample on-site parking for up to 100 vehicles with security and attendants provided.
